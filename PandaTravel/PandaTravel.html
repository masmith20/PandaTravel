What to do in Xochicalco, Mexico

Xochicalco was said to be inhabited around 650 AD to 900 AD. After 900 AD, the city was suddenly abandoned, and why it was abandoned, along with who lived there, is still a mystery today.

In Xochicalco, you are given the opportunity to explore this city and its beautiful views, architecture, and the several different cultures it exhibits throughout its structure and architecture.
